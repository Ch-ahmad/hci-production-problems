Noto Sherif & Sanchez

Description

  • Noto Serif
    • Noto is a global font collection for writing in all modern and ancient languages. Noto Serif is a modulated (“serif”) design for texts in the Latin, Cyrillic and Greek scripts, also suitable as the complementary font for other script-specific Noto Serif fonts.
  • Sanchez
    • Sanchez is a slab serif typeface family from Chilean type foundry LatinoType.
